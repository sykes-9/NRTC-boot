DFATD funded Nutrition Links project

This is collaborative involving the University of Ghana, McGill University, Heifer International, and World Vision International to undertake studies on building capacity for sustainable livelihoods through Agriculture and health and nutrition linkages. The project was funded by DFATD (former CIDA, Canada). The Centre provided office space for this project, as well as the use of the demonstration kitchen and conference room (when available) for the project’s activities. The Centre also hosted the project’s demonstration garden and poultry.
